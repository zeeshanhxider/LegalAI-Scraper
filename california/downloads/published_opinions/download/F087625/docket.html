03/08/2024 Order filed. The record on appeal has not been received within the timeframe prescribed by law. Accordingly, within 30 days of the date of this order, the Court Executive Officer of the Kern County Superior Court is directed to take one of the following actions:

(1) Send the original appellate records to the Clerk/Executive Officer of this Court, including any reporter's transcripts properly certified (See Cal. Rules of Court, rule 8.150(a));

(2) Secure the stenographic notes of any delinquent certified shorthand reporter and arrange for them to be transcribed and certified by an alternate certified shorthand reporter. Send the original appellate records to the Clerk/Executive Officer of this Court, including the reporter's transcripts certified by the alternate certified shorthand reporter (See Cal. Rules of Court, rule 8.150(a); see also People v. Chessman (1950) 35 Cal.2d 455, 459, 461[transcription and certification of reporter's transcripts by a certified shorthand reporter based on the stenographic notes of another reporter]);

(3) Request an extension of time from this court for the transcription and certification of either the reporter's or clerk's transcripts, detailing the steps taken to date and the current status of transcript preparation in each case (see Cal. Rules of Court, rules 8.60(c); 8.130(f)(1); 8.336(e), (h); or

(4) Notify this court and all parties that specific portions of the proceedings cannot be transcribed, in which case the appeals will proceed by way of agreed statements or settled statements as to those portions of the proceedings (see Cal. Rules of Court, rules 8.130(g)(1); 8.134, 8.137, 8.344, 8.346; see generally People v. Chessman, supra, 35 Cal.2d 455).

Failure to comply with this order could result in further actions by this court including any actions authorized pursuant to California Rules of Court, Rule 8.23.
