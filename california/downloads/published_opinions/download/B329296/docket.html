09/24/2025 Order filed. This Court is in receipt of appellant's September 16, 2025 letter brief transmitting a copy of the superior court's September 3, 2025 ruling on appellant's August 1, 2025 motion for release on bail pending appeal. To the extent the letter brief purports to seek review of the superior court's September 3, 2025 order, any such relief must be sought via the filing of a motion in this Court. We note appellant's letter brief suggests that this Court's August 23, 2023 order denying his first motion for bail on appeal without prejudice should be interpreted to have limited the scope of the superior court's consideration of appellant's current motion for bail on appeal. This Court denied appellant's renewed motion for bail on appeal on April 3, 2024. On July 3, 2025, this Court denied appellant's third motion for bail on appeal without prejudice to appellant's filing a new motion in the superior court. Neither this Court's April 3, 2024 order nor the July 3, 2025 order contained any limiting language directing the contents or scope of any subsequent motions or filings or any limit on the superior court's decisions in any further proceedings, and no such limitation is or was intended by any of this Court's orders.
