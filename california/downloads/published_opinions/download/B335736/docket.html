10/08/2024 Filed order consolidating with case: The Court has read and considered the parties' Joint Motion to Consolidate Appeal Nos B335736, B338256, and B339211, filed September 25, 2024. It is hereby ordered that the above appeals are consolidated for the purposes of briefing, oral argument, and decision. All future filings shall be made under B335736.

Appellant Patrick Ryan (Ryan) shall file his opening brief and appendix by November 4, 2024. Cross-appellants and respondents Maynard Matthews and Tanis Matthews (collectively, the Matthews) shall file the combined respondents' brief and opening briefs and appendix 90 days after the date Ryan files his opening brief. Ryan shall have the stipulated 60 days to file the combined reply brief and respondent's brief, so that the total time for filing this brief shall be 90 days after the date of the Matthews filing of their combined respondents' and opening brief. The Matthews shall have the stipulated time of 60 days to file their reply brief, so that the total time for filing this brief shall be 80 days after the date Ryan files his combined appellant's reply and respondent's brief.

The parties are not precluded from requesting additional time from the Court.
