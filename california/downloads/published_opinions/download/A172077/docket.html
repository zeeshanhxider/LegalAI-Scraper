01/21/2026 Order filed. BY THE COURT:

The court is in receipt of a letter indicating that a Chapter 11 bankruptcy petition was filed on September 5, 2025, concerning appellant Navellier & Associates, Inc. The notice does not comply with the applicable local rule prescribing the form of notice for advising the court of the existence and scope of a bankruptcy stay. (See Ct. App. First Dist., Local Rules of Ct., rule 21(b).)

Counsel for appellants is directed to file an amended notice that includes (1) the bankruptcy petition; (2) a copy of the most recent order of the bankruptcy court and of any stay order issued by that court; and (3) an explanation of whether a stay order is in effect and why the stay applies to the pending appeal by noon today, January 21, 2026. Any party disputing the notice shall serve and file an opposing statement by 2:00 p.m. today.
