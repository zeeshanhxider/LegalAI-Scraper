TO: Edward Mahler, Esq.

Your letter pursuant to Rule 8.340 requesting an augmentation of the record on appeal was received. Rule 8.340 permits augmentation of the record on appeal without a court order where normal record was inadvertently omitted from the transcript.

The Court of Appeal, Second Appellate District, expands the normal record on appeal pursuant to Local Rules 1 and 2.

For cases where the document requested is not described in California Rules of Court, Rule 8.320, or in the local appellate rules, an augmentation order issued by the Court of Appeal is required.

- - Other: The request for original sentencing proceedings conducted on 02/27/1995 were previously requested to reporter service. The reporter may not keep records of proceedings for more than 10 years and may prepare an Affidavit.

Therefore, we are unable to process your request at this time.
