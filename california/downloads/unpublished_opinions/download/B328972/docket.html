03/24/2025 Order filed. THE COURT:
On February 22, 2024, this court ordered the superior court to set a hearing to settle the record on appeal. By motion filed March 6, 2025, appellant's counsel advised that the hearing to settle the record has not been set.
Accordingly, good cause appearing, Appellant's Motion To Reissue Order Granting Appellant's Motion to Settle the Record is granted. It is ordered that within 30 days from the date of this order, the matter be set for a record-settlement hearing in Department EAN, East District of the Los Angeles County Superior Court before Judge David C. Brougham and that the trial attorneys, deputy district attorney Lance Hansen, and appellant's trial counsel Jonathan Evans, attend the hearing with a settled statement then prepared and filed.
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that within 30 days of the hearing, the superior
court certify and file the settled record.
Counsel for appellant shall file a report regarding the status of the settled record within 60 days of this order.
