01/22/2026 Issued tentative opinion or disputed issued memo This case is scheduled for oral argument on the court's March 12, 2026 calendar.

The court is distributing the attached tentative opinion to the parties before the argument. The court believes the record and briefs satisfactorily present the facts and the controlling legal authority, and it is prepared to rule along the lines set forth in the tentative opinion.

If a party that previously requested argument notifies the court, opposing counsel, and any unrepresented party in writing by noon on February 2, 2026, that it still wishes to proceed with oral argument, oral argument will take place as scheduled and the views expressed in the tentative opinion will be subject to change. Notification must be made through the court's electronic filing system (TrueFiling).

A separate response to this Notice is required if a party that previously requested oral argument still wishes to proceed with argument. Filing the Request for Oral Argument form in response to the court's Notice of Oral Argument Election, or filing the Oral Argument Appearance Type form in response to the court's Oral Argument Notice, will not be considered a response to this Notice.

If no such notification is given, oral argument will not be held, and the court's final opinion will reflect the substance of the tentative opinion.
02/03/2026 Note: Assertion of oral argument in light of the tentative opinion was due 2/2/26. None submitted.
02/03/2026 Order filed. BY THE COURT:

The parties having been noticed of the requirement to assert oral argument after the issuance of the tentative opinion, if still desired, and no assertion having been made, the matter is vacated from the court's March 12, 2026 oral argument calendar.
02/04/2026 Opinion filed. (Signed Unpublished) The judgment entered following the grant of summary judgment in the defendants' favor is affirmed.
