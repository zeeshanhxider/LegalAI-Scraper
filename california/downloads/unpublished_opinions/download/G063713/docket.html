03/26/2025 *** Motion/Request Deferred *** On March 10, 2025, appellant filed a third request for judicial notice. (Cal. Rules
of Court, rule 8.252(a).) On March 12, 2025, appellant filed a fourth request for judicial
notice. On March 25, 2025, respondent filed opposition to the third and fourth requests
for judicial notice. Also on March 25, 2025, appellant filed a motion to file a reply in
support of the requests. The motion is GRANTED. The clerk of this court is DIRECTED
to file the reply received on March 25, 2025.
The third and fourth requests for judicial notice will be decided in conjunction
with the decision on appeal.
On January 23, 2025, this court entered an order regarding appellant's first two
requests for judicial notice (filed on Jan. 6, 2025). The court's order accurately stated that
the requests for judicial notice would be decided in conjunction with the decision on
appeal. Unfortunately, due to a clerical error, the order also stated that the "request for
judicial notice is GRANTED." The January 23, 2025, order is VACATED solely to the
extent it purported to grant a prior request for judicial notice. All four requests for judicial
notice filed by appellant will be decided in conjunction with the decision on appeal.Emailed and mailed to appellant via USPS.

Each side is allowed up to 15 minutes for oral argument. Where there are
more than two parties, a side consists of all parties whose interests are not adverse.

apportion or expand the time according to the respective interests of the parties and
