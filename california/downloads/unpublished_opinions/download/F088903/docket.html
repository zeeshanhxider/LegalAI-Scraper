07/24/2025 Augmentation granted. (See order.) On June 18, 2025, respondent filed a "MOTION TO AUGMENT," requesting copies of the CD exhibits containing police body camera footage relied upon by the juvenile court (exhibit Nos. 1 and 2). On June 20, 2025, this court directed the Clerk of the Tulare County Superior Court (Clerk) to provide an augmented record of copies of exhibits Nos. 1 and 2. On July 8, 2025, this court received a declaration from the Clerk stating that exhibits No. 1 and 2 were returned to the People pursuant to the minute order of the hearing dated October 10, 2024. According to the record on appeal, exhibit Nos. 1 and 2 were received and filed by the superior court and were required to be retained by the superior court.

In response to the clerk's declaration, on July 18, 2025, respondent filed a second "MOTION TO AUGMENT &," (second motion) requesting that the court obtain the missing exhibits from the parties. Good cause appearing, respondent's second motion is granted. The Tulare Superior Court is directed to obtain a copy of exhibit Nos. 1 and 2 that were provided to the court in the above entitle case from the party who submitted the exhibits. Upon receipt of the exhibits, the superior court is directed to mark the exhibits and prepare a settled statement stating whether the exhibits are the same as the ones provided to the court in the above entitled case. The Clerk is directed to cause the exhibits and the settled statement to be prepared and certified, and to transmit them to this court and to counsel within 30 days of the date of this order.

Time to file respondent's brief is hereby extended to and including 30 days from the date of the filing of the augmentation ordered above.
