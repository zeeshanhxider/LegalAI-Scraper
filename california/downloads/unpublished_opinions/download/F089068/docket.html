09/02/2025 Notice re Delgadillo. The attorney appointed to represent you on your appeal to this court has filed an opening brief pursuant to People v. Wende (1979) 25 Cal.3d 436. The brief sets out the applicable facts and law but states that the attorney was unable to find any arguable issues on appeal. In lieu of this court providing you a copy of this brief, your attorney is ordered to provide you with a copy of this brief.

Previously, when an appeal was taken from the denial of a petition for postconviction relief under Penal Code section 1172.6 (formerly Pen. Code section 1170.95) and counsel filed a brief under People v. Wende, supra, 25 Cal.3d 436, this court performed an independent review of the record to determine whether any error occurred. Our Supreme Court has determined that independent Wende review is not required for appeals from the denial of Penal Code section 1172.6 petitions. (People v. Delgadillo (2022) 14 Cal.5th 216.)

This order serves as a notice that you have 30 days from the date of this order to file a supplemental letter or brief on your own behalf raising any arguable issues. If this court does not receive a supplemental letter or brief from you within the time specified in this order, the appeal may be dismissed as abandoned. (People v. Delgadillo, supra, 14 Cal.5th 216.)
