02/04/2026 Opinion filed. Our May 19, 2025 decision is vacated. Pursuant to Fletcher, supra, 18 Cal.5th 576, the true findings on the strike and the prior serious felony enhancement are reversed. Appellant's sentence is vacated. The matter is remanded to the
trial court with directions that the People be allowed to elect whether to retry appellant, under current law, on the strike and the prior serious felony enhancement. We also remand the matter for a full resentencing. In all other respects, the judgment is affirmed.
