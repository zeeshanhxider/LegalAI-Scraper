If a party that previously requested argument notifies the court, opposing counsel, and any unrepresented party in writing by 11:00 a.m. on by noon on January 15, 2026, that it still wishes to proceed with oral argument, oral argument will take place as scheduled and the views expressed in the tentative opinion will be subject to change.
